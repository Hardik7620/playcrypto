Playcrypto365 Privacy Policy

Who We Are—And Why That Matters
Most privacy policies begin with legalese. But let’s start with something simpler: trust. PlayCrypto365 is not just a gaming platform; it’s a system built on your trust. Every time you tap a button, verify your account, or deposit a digital token, you’re giving us more than data—you’re handing over a piece of your digital self. That’s a big deal.

Our operator? Twocent Technology Limited, based in Belize. Our license? Fully compliant under the Government of Curaçao. But our commitment? To you.

This isn’t the story of boring documents. This is a story about how digital footprints define the world you interact with, and how one company—PlayCrypto365—chooses to respect that.


Why We Collect Data: It’s Not Just About Numbers

The Legal Foundations
Every platform collects data. What matters is why. At PlayCrypto365, our data collection is backed by legal and ethical reasoning:

  • Performance of a Contract – You can’t have a functioning gaming experience if we don’t know who you are or what you’ve done on the platform.

  • Legitimate Interests – Like stopping fraud before it starts, helping you when things go wrong, or making the games smoother and smarter.

  • Compliance – Governments require certain actions for taxes, anti-money laundering (AML), and so forth. We comply.

  • Consent – And when the law isn’t clear? We’ll ask you. Directly.

What We Actually Collect
Let’s break it down:

  • Account Details: Your PlayCrypto365 ID, email, age, username, and whether your account is active.

  • Contact Info: Name, number, address, email.

  • Device Details: IP address, the type of device you’re using, your browser, operating system.

  • Money Movement: Every deposit, every withdrawal, every payment method you’ve used.

  • Location Info: Sometimes it’s approximate. Sometimes, if you enable it, it’s precise.

  • Government IDs: Only when needed—typically for Know Your Customer (KYC) rules.

  • Interaction Logs: What you clicked, when you logged in, how long you played.

  • Security Tags: Device trust score, how often something unusual happens.

  • Marketing Preferences: Whether you want newsletters or promotional offers.

Here’s what we don’t collect:  we don’t collect your faith, your political beliefs. Also, your sexual identity. Unless the law demands it, and unless you explicitly say yes, we stay away.


How We Use It: Function Over Fancy

We don’t sit on your data like it’s gold. We use it to:

  • Keep your experience smooth

  • Keep your account secure

  • Process payments

  • Customize your dashboard

  • Prevent fraud

  • Meet regulatory and tax requirements

  • Personalize ads and offers (only with permission)

  • Handle your support tickets with context

  • Enforce fair play rules

We also use it for special programs—like VIP access or rewards. If you're in, your data helps power that engine.


Your Data May Travel, But It's Never Unprotected

We’re a global platform. That means your data might move beyond borders. 

When that happens, we don’t just ship it across and hope for the best. We use legally binding tools:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

  • Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

  • Encryption and secure transfers

No matter where your data goes, it’s still governed by strong digital guardrails.


How Long Do We Hold Onto Your Info?

Nothing lasts forever—except maybe blockchain. Your data? It has an expiry date.

  • Account Info – As long as your account is active

  • Financial Records – 7 years, per tax and compliance laws

  • Security Data – Up to 5 years

  • Marketing Preferences – Until you say "no more"

Sometimes we’re required to keep a sliver longer, but we never stretch it beyond what the law allows.


Know Your Rights: You’re Not Powerless

Your data belongs to you. And depending on where you live, these rights are yours:

  • Right to Access – You can ask for what we’ve got on file.

  • Right to Correct – See a mistake? Let us fix it.

  • Right to Delete – Say the word and, if possible, it’s gone.

  • Right to Restrict Use – Want us to hold off? We’ll pause processing under certain legal limits.

  • Right to Object – Especially for marketing.

  • Right to Move It Elsewhere – We’ll package it up for you.

  • Right to Complain – Speak to your national authority.

We make these processes easy and human—not buried in forms.


Security: Our Invisible Armor

You won’t see the layers, but you should know they’re there:

  • SSL encryption when data moves

  • Multi-Factor Authentication for sensitive logins

  • Limited access to personal data—only trained staff can view it

  • Annual third-party audits

  • Breach alerts when needed

  • Strict compliance with ISO 27001 and SOC 2

  • Regular training for our internal teams

In short, your data isn’t sitting in a drawer. It’s protected in motion and at rest.


No Kids Allowed: Age Restrictions Are Real

We don’t accept users under 18. No exceptions. If we detect someone has signed up underage, we immediately delete their info and ban the account.


Who Else Sees Your Info?

We only share data when it’s necessary—and only with partners who have agreed to protect it:

  • Affiliates within our group

  • Payment providers

  • Cloud services

  • KYC & fraud prevention tools

  • Law enforcement, when legally demanded

We don’t sell or rent your data to third parties. No backroom deals. No trading info to marketers.

If our company ever merges or gets acquired, your data goes along—but always under the same rules.


Marketing: We Ask First

We send newsletters, promos, and game invites—but only if you’ve given us the green light. You can say no anytime by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe"

  • Changing preferences in your dashboard

No begging. No spam. Just options.


Regulators & Recourse

If you’re in the EEA, or anywhere with a data protection agency, and you feel something’s off, go ahead and report us. We’ll cooperate. We won’t hide.

We prefer to resolve things before it comes to that, but your rights matter more than our convenience.


We Update Our Policy, But Never In Secret

Laws change. Technology evolves. We rewrite parts of our policy accordingly. But we always:

  • Post the update on our website

  • Notify you directly via email or app notifications if it's major

You’ll never have to dig around for the latest version.


One Last Thing

We don’t view privacy as a checkbox. We view it as a promise. When you enter the world of PlayCrypto365, we’re not just welcoming a user—we’re taking responsibility for their digital shadow.

So read this policy, not as an obligation, but as a handshake.

Welcome to the game. Your privacy matters here.
